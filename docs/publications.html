Aadarsh Padiyath, Brooke Compton, Barbara Ericson, Mark Guzdial (2026)
ACM Conference on Innovation and Technology in Computer Science Education
Finds that while academic literature frames adversarial thinking as open-ended creative exploration, cybersecurity professionals under real time constraints instead rely on a risk-based mindset and institutionalized adversarial knowledge, deploying deeper creative analysis only when standardized checklists fall short.
